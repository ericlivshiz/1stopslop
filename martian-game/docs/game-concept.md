# Martian Game — Concept

## Pitch

4–8 astronauts crash on a hostile planet. Nobody knows how to do everything. Every player has a profession. The planet constantly throws disasters at the crew. If the crew survives long enough, they escape.

**Think:** Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes, Lethal Company, Among Us, Barotrauma  
**Not:** Minecraft, Rust, Valheim

---

## Core Fantasy

Specialized roles + constant pressure + forced communication.

Every player only has **partial information**. The Engineer sees the power graph. The Botanist sees plant disease. The Medic sees blood infection. The Pilot sees incoming weather. Nobody else can. That is why everyone matters.

---

## Core Gameplay Loop

Every “day” lasts about **5–10 minutes**.

```
Explore
  ↓
Collect resources
  ↓
Maintain base
  ↓
Solve disasters
  ↓
Research technology
  ↓
Night comes
  ↓
New catastrophe
  ↓
Repeat
```

If the crew survives **X days**, they escape.

Keep the game moving. Every minute something happens. Do not make players chop trees for 45 minutes or craft 500 items.

---

## Professions

Every player specializes. No one person can do everything.

| Profession | Owns |
|---|---|
| **Botanist** | Food, oxygen plants |
| **Engineer** | Repairs, generators |
| **Chemist** | Medicine, fuel |
| **Geologist** | Mining, scans |
| **Pilot** | Communications, navigation |
| **Medic** | Healing |

### Example table talk

- **Engineer:** “Generator’s about to explode.”
- **Botanist:** “I’m literally suffocating trying to keep oxygen alive.”
- **Pilot:** “There’s a dust storm in 90 seconds.”
- **Medic:** “Eric stop eating the emergency rations.”

That is good multiplayer.

## Role Minigames

Do not make players hold E. Every role has tiny games.

### Botany
- Identify disease
- Splice DNA
- Pollinate
- Balance nutrients

### Engineering
- Reroute power
- Solder wires
- Fix circuits
- Pressure control

### Mining
- Scan terrain
- Choose drill angle
- Stabilize tunnel

### Medicine
- Diagnose diseases
- Surgery
- Antidotes

### Chemistry
- Combine chemicals
- Produce oxygen
- Make batteries

---

## Constant Pressure

The game should always be yelling at players.

Example disasters:
- Meteor shower
- Radiation spike
- Sandstorm
- Alien spores
- Solar flare
- Water contamination
- Power outage
- Hull breach
- Freezing temperatures
- Heat wave
- Oxygen leak
- Disease outbreak
- Food rot

Every disaster forces communication.

---

## Interconnected Resources

No generic wood/stone loops. Everything depends on someone else.

```
Iron
  ↓
Engineer builds extractor
  ↓
Extractor uses fuel
  ↓
Fuel made by Chemist
  ↓
Fuel requires algae
  ↓
Botanist grows algae
  ↓
Plants need water
  ↓
Water extracted by Miner
```

Everything loops.

---

## Death Should Be Funny

Nobody should just lose HP.

Examples:
- You slowly suffocate
- You hallucinate
- You become radiation sick
- You catch alien fungus
- You freeze solid
- You overheat
- Your suit runs out of oxygen

This creates hilarious clips.

---

## Funny Social Mechanics

All accidental (not grief-first design):

- Accidentally vent all oxygen
- Someone steals food
- Someone forgets batteries
- Someone locks everyone outside
- Someone drives rover into lava
- Someone eats poisonous mushrooms
- Someone floods greenhouse
- Someone destroys reactor

## Exploration

Procedurally generated map with biomes such as:
- Crystal caves
- Acid lakes
- Frozen valleys
- Volcanoes
- Alien ruins
- Old colony
- Meteor craters
- Underground tunnels

---

## Alien Life

Not necessarily combat-first.

Maybe: worms, slime, plants, floating jellyfish, microbes, predators.

Some helpful. Some dangerous.

---

## Progression (Meta)

Instead of XP grind, unlock:
- New professions
- New maps
- Harder disasters
- Cosmetics
- New technologies
- Challenge modifiers

---

## Difficulty Scaling

| Mode | Feel |
|---|---|
| **Easy** | Long oxygen, gentle weather |
| **Hard** | Random disasters, equipment failures, diseases |
| **Nightmare** | Everything breaks constantly |

---

## Partial Information (Non-Negotiable)

| Role | Unique view |
|---|---|
| Engineer | Power graph |
| Botanist | Plant disease |
| Medic | Blood infection |
| Pilot | Incoming weather |

If everyone can see everything, the game dies.

- “THE GREENHOUSE IS ON FIRE”

- “THE REACTOR’S MELTING”

---

## Design North Star

1. Communication is the real skill.
2. Specialization creates dependence.
3. Disasters create urgency.
4. Failure is funny, not punishing in a dull way.
5. Keep sessions short and chaotic.
